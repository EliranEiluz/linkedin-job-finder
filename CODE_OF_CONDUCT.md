# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)** as its code of conduct. The full text governs participation in this project's spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channels associated with the repository.

By participating, you agree to uphold the standards outlined there. Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards, and may take any action they deem appropriate, including removing comments, commits, or contributors.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact **eiluz.eliran7@gmail.com**. Reports are handled confidentially and reviewed promptly.

For details on what to include and how reports are handled, see the [Enforcement section of the Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement).
